OTONAL-INVERSE

[GENERIC-FUNCTION]

Function arguments:

  - OTONAL otonal chord [default = (1 3/2 5/4)]

Description:

It gives the utonal chord of a otonal chord. In other words, intervals ascendants become intervals descent.
