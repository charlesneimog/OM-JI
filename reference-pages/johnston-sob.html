JOHNSTON-SOB

[GENERIC-FUNCTION]
OM-JI
Function Reference
ARGUMENTS:
  - ratio first ratio [default = 3/2]
  - sobreposition sobreposition number [default = 3]
  - fundamental fundamental [default = 7200]

Description:

Gilmore (1995, p. 477) will suggest that Johnston's system is summarizable to a stacking of just intervals above and below the fundamental pitch. This object do this. In first inlet are the ratio that will be stacking. In second inlet the number of stackings above and below. In third inlet the fundamnetal pitch in midicents.
